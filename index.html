About Yo Jobs

Redefining How Companies
Find Great People

Founded in 2016, Yo Jobs has grown from a small placement firm to one of Pakistan's most trusted HR and recruitment agencies. We blend deep industry knowledge with a human-first approach to find the right fit — not just the right resume.

Whether you're a startup scaling fast or an enterprise optimizing your workforce, our team of certified HR professionals is committed to delivering results that matter.

  • Industry-certified HR consultants with domain expertise
  • Tailored recruitment strategies for every client
  • Transparent process with real-time candidate tracking
  • Post-placement support and 90-day replacement guarantee
